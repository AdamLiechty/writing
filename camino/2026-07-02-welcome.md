Buen Camino! On August 1, I'll be starting a 500-mile walk along the French Way of the [Camino de Santiago](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Camino_de_Santiago).

During the trip, I'll post updates [here on PolarSteps](https://www.polarsteps.com/AdamLiechty/27045093-a-new-adventure?s=4abc4139-a2f6-4b96-ba1d-58565a9f3526), and afterward on this blog. You don't need an account on PolarSteps to see updates, but if you want the more interactive experience, you can get the app and "follow" my trip. Adieu and adios!
